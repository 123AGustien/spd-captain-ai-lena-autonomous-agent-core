# FIN-005 — Systemic Financial Stress

## Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-005
- Domain: Financial Resilience
- Category: Systemic Financial Stability
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library

## Purpose

Evaluate systemic financial stress resulting from simultaneous or cascading disruptions across interconnected financial domains.

## Scenario

Multiple financial stress conditions occur simultaneously or sequentially, creating the potential for systemic instability.

## Trigger Conditions

- Combined FX and liquidity stress
- Banking system instability
- Market-wide volatility
- Credit deterioration
- Cross-domain financial contagion
- Multiple simultaneous financial disruptions

## Risk Assessment

Assess:

1. FX stress
2. Market stress
3. Liquidity stress
4. Banking stress
5. Credit conditions
6. Interconnected dependencies
7. Cascade intensity
8. Systemic resilience

## Decision Logic

### LOW

Maintain monitoring and normal resilience operations.

### MEDIUM

Activate coordinated preventive resilience measures.

### HIGH

Activate systemic stabilization mode and prioritize critical financial functions.

## Recommended Actions

1. Consolidate all available financial risk indicators.
2. Verify independent data sources.
3. Identify primary and secondary stress drivers.
4. Map potential cascade pathways.
5. Protect critical liquidity and financial reserves.
6. Prioritize essential financial operations.
7. Monitor banking and market stability.
8. Reassess system state after each intervention.
9. Escalate stabilization measures when required.

## Golden Rule

OBSERVE → VERIFY → ASSESS → DECIDE → ACT → UPDATE

## Expected Outcome

Detect systemic financial stress early, reduce cascade propagation, protect critical financial functions, and support resilient decision-making.
