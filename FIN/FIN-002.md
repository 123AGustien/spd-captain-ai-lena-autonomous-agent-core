FIN-002 — Financial Stress

Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-002
- Domain: FIN
- Domain Name: Financial Resilience
- Category: Financial System Resilience
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library
- Scenario ID: FINANCIAL_STRESS

---

Purpose

Evaluate the resilience of a financial system during periods of broad financial stress affecting markets, institutions, funding conditions, liquidity, asset values, and confidence.

The rule identifies systemic financial pressure and provides a structured resilience response through the SPD v13.1 Sextant Golden Rule Engine.

---

Scenario

A broad financial stress event develops across the financial system.

The scenario may involve simultaneous pressure across:

- Financial markets
- Banking institutions
- Liquidity conditions
- Credit availability
- Asset valuations
- Foreign exchange markets
- External funding
- Investor confidence
- Energy and infrastructure financing
- Cross-domain financial stability

The scenario is designed to evaluate whether financial stress remains contained or begins to propagate across interconnected systems.

Decision Logic

LOW Risk

If the calculated risk remains LOW, the financial system remains within controlled resilience conditions.

Recommended operating approach:

CONTINUE CONTROLLED OPERATIONS
MAINTAIN NORMAL MONITORING
PRESERVE FINANCIAL RESILIENCE

If energy resilience is also low, Captain AI Lena may select:

ENERGY PROTECTION MODE

Recommended action:

REDUCE SYSTEM LOAD AND PRESERVE ENERGY RESERVES

---

MEDIUM Risk

If the calculated risk is MEDIUM:

PREVENTIVE RESILIENCE MODE

Recommended approach:

INCREASE FINANCIAL SYSTEM MONITORING
PROTECT LIQUIDITY
REDUCE EXCESSIVE RISK EXPOSURE
REVIEW CREDIT CONDITIONS
MONITOR MARKET CONFIDENCE
REVIEW FOREIGN CURRENCY EXPOSURE
PREPARE CONTINGENCY ACTIONS

---

HIGH Risk

If the calculated risk is HIGH:

ACTIVATE STABILIZATION MODE

Recommended approach:

PROTECT CRITICAL FINANCIAL FUNCTIONS
PRESERVE LIQUIDITY
STABILIZE ESSENTIAL OPERATIONS
REDUCE SYSTEMIC FINANCIAL EXPOSURE
MONITOR BANKING CONDITIONS
MONITOR MARKET AND CREDIT CONDITIONS
MONITOR CASCADE CONDITIONS
PREPARE ESCALATION RESPONSE
