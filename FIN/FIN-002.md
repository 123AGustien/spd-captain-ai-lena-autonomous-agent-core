# FIN-002 — Financial Market Stress

## Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-002
- Domain: Financial Resilience
- Category: Market Stability
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library

## Purpose

Evaluate systemic resilience during periods of financial market instability and identify potential cascading effects across financial and operational systems.

## Scenario

Financial markets experience elevated volatility, declining confidence, reduced liquidity, and increased risk of systemic contagion.

## Trigger Conditions

- Market volatility increase
- Asset price instability
- Reduced market liquidity
- Credit spread widening
- Loss of investor confidence
- Increased systemic risk indicators

## Risk Assessment

Assess:

1. Market volatility
2. Liquidity conditions
3. Credit conditions
4. Counterparty exposure
5. Confidence indicators
6. Interconnected system dependencies

## Decision Logic

### LOW

Maintain normal monitoring.

### MEDIUM

Activate preventive resilience measures and increase monitoring frequency.

### HIGH

Activate stabilization mode and protect critical financial functions.

## Recommended Actions

1. Increase monitoring of market conditions.
2. Assess liquidity availability.
3. Review counterparty exposure.
4. Protect critical financial operations.
5. Reduce unnecessary risk concentration.
6. Evaluate systemic cascade pathways.
7. Prepare contingency measures.

## Golden Rule

OBSERVE → VERIFY → ASSESS → DECIDE → ACT → UPDATE

## Expected Outcome

Reduce the probability of financial market stress propagating into wider systemic instability.
