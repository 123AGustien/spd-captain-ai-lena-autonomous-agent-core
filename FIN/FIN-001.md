FIN-001 — Foreign Exchange Stress

Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-001
- Domain: FIN
- Domain Name: Financial Resilience
- Category: Foreign Exchange Resilience
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library
- Scenario ID: FX_SHOCK

---

Purpose

Evaluate the resilience of a financial system when exposed to significant foreign exchange volatility, currency depreciation, exchange-rate shocks, or rapid changes in external funding conditions.

The rule identifies potential financial stress arising from foreign exchange movements and provides a structured resilience response through the SPD v13.1 Sextant Golden Rule Engine.

---

Scenario

A sudden foreign exchange shock causes increased currency volatility and financial pressure.

The scenario may affect:

- Foreign currency exposure
- Import costs
- External debt servicing
- Liquidity conditions
- Energy costs
- Banking stability
- Infrastructure financing
- Market confidence
- Cross-domain financial resilience

The appropriate solution must be determined from the assessed system state.

4. Protect available liquidity
5. Reduce unnecessary financial exposure
6. Review external funding requirements
7. Monitor energy and infrastructure impacts
8. Monitor banking and systemic contagion indicators
9. Apply the selected mitigation
10. Monitor system response
