# FIN-001 — Foreign Exchange Stress

## Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-001
- Domain: Financial Resilience
- Category: Foreign Exchange Stability
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library

## Purpose

Evaluate the impact of foreign exchange volatility on system resilience, liquidity, operational stability, and interconnected financial dependencies.

## Scenario

The system experiences a rapid deterioration in foreign exchange conditions, creating pressure on funding costs, import dependency, liquidity, and financial stability.

## Trigger Conditions

- Significant FX volatility
- Rapid currency depreciation
- Increased foreign currency funding pressure
- Import cost escalation
- Reduced foreign exchange liquidity

## Risk Assessment

Assess:

1. FX volatility intensity
2. Currency exposure
3. Foreign currency liquidity
4. Import dependency
5. Banking system sensitivity
6. Cross-domain cascade potential

## Decision Logic

### LOW

Continue monitoring.

### MEDIUM

Activate preventive financial resilience measures.

### HIGH

Activate financial stabilization measures and preserve liquidity.

## Recommended Actions

1. Monitor FX movements continuously.
2. Assess foreign currency liquidity.
3. Reduce unnecessary foreign currency exposure.
4. Review import-dependent critical operations.
5. Evaluate banking and liquidity stress.
6. Activate contingency funding measures if required.
7. Monitor cascade effects across connected domains.

## Golden Rule

OBSERVE → VERIFY → ASSESS → DECIDE → ACT → UPDATE

## Expected Outcome

Maintain financial resilience and prevent FX stress from propagating into critical operational systems.
