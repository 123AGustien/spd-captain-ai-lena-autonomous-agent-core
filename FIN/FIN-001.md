FIN-001 — FX Stress (SGD/IDR)

Rule Information

Rule ID: FIN-001
Domain: Financial Resilience
Category: Foreign Exchange (FX)
Status: Active
Version: 1.0
Repository: Sextant Rule Library

Purpose

Evaluate the resilience of the financial system during periods of significant SGD/IDR exchange rate volatility and simulate potential cascading effects across connected domains.

Scenario

A sudden increase in foreign capital outflows causes pressure on the Indonesian Rupiah against the Singapore Dollar.

The scenario evaluates:

- Exchange rate volatility
- Liquidity conditions
- Inflation pressure
- Interest rate pressure
- Financial market confidence

Primary Indicators

- SGD/IDR exchange rate
- Daily FX volatility
- Capital inflow/outflow trends
- Foreign reserve pressure
- Government bond market activity
- Central bank policy actions

Risk Levels

GREEN

Normal market conditions.

FX movements remain within expected trading ranges.

No contingency actions required.

YELLOW

Early signs of increased FX volatility.

Monitor:

- Exchange rate movement
- Liquidity conditions
- Bond market activity

Action:

Increase monitoring frequency.

ORANGE

Sustained currency pressure.

Indicators:

- Rapid depreciation
- Increased capital outflows
- Rising market uncertainty

Potential Cascade:

FX Stress
↓
Inflation Pressure
↓
Interest Rate Pressure
↓
Liquidity Tightening

Action:

Prepare contingency measures and strengthen monitoring.

RED

Severe foreign exchange instability.

Indicators:

- Extreme exchange rate volatility
- Significant capital flight
- Liquidity deterioration
- Broad financial market stress

Potential Cascade:

FX Crisis
↓
Banking Stress
↓
Corporate Funding Stress
↓
Cross-Domain Systemic Risk

Action:

Activate emergency response procedures and systemic risk management protocols.

Cross-Domain Dependencies

Financial (FIN)
↓
Infrastructure (INF)
↓
Cyber (CYB)
↓
Data Centre Operations (DC)

Financial instability may increase operational stress across interconnected domains.

Contingency Actions

- Monitor FX market continuously
- Assess liquidity conditions
- Review funding exposure
- Evaluate systemic risk propagation
- Increase reporting frequency
- Escalate to institutional risk management when thresholds are exceeded

Simulation Output

- Risk Level
- Cascade Path
- Affected Domains
- Recommended Contingency Actions
- Audit Log Entry
