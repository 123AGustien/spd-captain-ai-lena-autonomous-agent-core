# FIN-003 — Liquidity Stress

## Rule Information

- Rule ID: FIN-003
- Domain: Financial Resilience
- Category: Liquidity Stability
- Status: Active
- Version: 1.0
- Repository: Sextant Rule Library

## Purpose

Evaluate liquidity resilience during periods of financial stress and determine whether available liquidity is sufficient to maintain critical operations.

## Scenario

The system experiences declining liquidity, increased funding pressure, reduced access to financing, or accelerated cash outflows.

## Trigger Conditions

- Rapid liquidity reduction
- Increased cash outflows
- Funding access deterioration
- Short-term funding pressure
- Reduced available reserves
- Banking sector liquidity concerns

## Risk Assessment

Assess:

1. Available liquidity
2. Cash flow pressure
3. Funding availability
4. Reserve adequacy
5. Banking system exposure
6. Critical operational funding requirements

## Decision Logic

### LOW

Continue normal liquidity monitoring.

### MEDIUM

Activate preventive liquidity preservation measures.

### HIGH

Activate liquidity stabilization mode and protect critical reserves.

## Recommended Actions

1. Monitor liquidity continuously.
2. Preserve critical cash reserves.
3. Review short-term funding requirements.
4. Reduce non-essential expenditure.
5. Prioritize critical operational funding.
6. Assess banking counterparties.
7. Prepare contingency funding arrangements.

## Golden Rule

OBSERVE → VERIFY → ASSESS → DECIDE → ACT → UPDATE

## Expected Outcome

Maintain sufficient liquidity to support critical operations and prevent liquidity stress from escalating into systemic financial instability.
